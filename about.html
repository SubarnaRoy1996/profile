About Dr. Subarna Roy

Computer Skills

  • Computational Software: MatCont, MATLAB, Maple
  • Typesetting Software: LaTeX, Microsoft Office

Research Interests

  • Mathematical modeling of ecological systems
  • Models with seasonality
  • Models with stochasticity

His research further focuses on the development and analysis of differential equation models to study the dynamics of ecosystems. He is also interested in exploring the effects of random environmental fluctuations and time delays on population dynamics.

Dr. Roy also collaborates on interdisciplinary projects that involve numerical simulations and real-world data to validate the theoretical models.
